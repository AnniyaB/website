• No midterm.
  • Final exam
    • 2 hours, open book, essay type questions (no MCQ), covers materials from all lectures, handouts, and tutorials.
    • The format is different from traditional exams. Samples: AY14/15 Sem 2 ( exam paper, comments about answers), AY14/15 Sem 1 (exam paper,  comments about answers), AY13/14 Sem 2 (exam paper,  comments about answers)  AY12/13-Sem1  AY11/12-Sem1.
    • Attend the last lecture to learn more about the exam paper.
    • There will be no model answers for past papers. However, we can give feedback for answers you come up with.
    • Exam consultations (consultations near the exam) are discouraged but allowed. We prefer you to learn and clarify along the way, not at the end.
    • Study tips:
      • Go through the worked examples in handouts
      • Learn all parts of the project, not just the part you did
      • Explore other extra features, not just the one your team did

Note that CS2103 paper measures how well you can apply the theory to a project. Therefore, the exam is strongly connected to the project and how you  applied theory in the project. The exam will be easy for you if you attend lectures, participate in tutorials (tutorials too are focused on application of theory to the project), and do a sincere job in the project. However, you will not be able to ace the exams by simply doing past papers just before the exams.
